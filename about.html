About Us

At A PLUS Driveways & Landscaping LTD, we take pride in transforming outdoor spaces into breathtaking landscapes. As a reputable limited company, we specialize in providing a wide range of top-quality landscaping services to enhance the beauty and functionality of residential and commercial properties. With a passion for creating outdoor environments that inspire and captivate, we offer an extensive array of services, including driveways, patios, fencing, turfing, and much more. Our team of highly skilled professionals brings years of experience and expertise to every project we undertake. We understand that each client has unique needs and preferences, which is why we take a personalized approach to ensure we exceed your expectations. Whether you desire a serene and elegant garden retreat, a stylish and inviting patio for entertaining guests, or a functional and durable driveway, we have the knowledge and resources to bring your vision to life.

  • Landscaping Services:
    Our comprehensive landscaping services encompass every aspect of outdoor design and maintenance. From concept development and site analysis to installation and ongoing care, we offer a holistic approach that covers everything from plant selection and hardscaping to irrigation systems and lighting solutions. Our team works closely with you to understand your aesthetic goals and create a customized design that harmonizes with the natural elements of your surroundings.

  • Driveways:
    We specialize in crafting stunning driveways that not only enhance the curb appeal of your property but also provide durable and functional surfaces. Whether you prefer classic paved driveways, decorative concrete, or permeable materials, our experts utilize the latest techniques and high-quality materials to create a driveway that suits your style and stands the test of time.

  • Patios:
    Our team excels in creating inviting and comfortable outdoor living spaces with our exceptional patio design and construction services. We work with various materials, including natural stone, pavers, and concrete, to deliver a visually striking and functional patio area that complements your lifestyle. From cozy seating areas to intricate patterns and eye-catching focal points, our designs are tailored to suit your individual taste and needs.

  • Fencing:
    Enhance the privacy, security, and aesthetic appeal of your property with our premium fencing solutions. Whether you prefer traditional wooden fences, contemporary metal options, or low-maintenance vinyl alternatives, we offer a range of styles and materials to match your preferences. Our skilled craftsmen ensure precision installation, delivering fences that are not only visually appealing but also built to last.

  • Turfing:
    Achieve a lush and vibrant lawn with our professional turfing services. From site preparation to soil analysis and selection of the right grass varieties, we meticulously install high-quality turf that transforms your outdoor space. We take care of all the details, ensuring proper irrigation, drainage, and maintenance, so you can enjoy a stunning and healthy lawn year-round.

At A PLUS Driveways & Landscaping LTD, we are committed to delivering exceptional results and unparalleled customer satisfaction. We strive for excellence in every project we undertake, no matter the size or complexity. Let our dedicated team of experts turn your outdoor dreams into a reality, and experience the transformative power of our landscaping services. Contact us today to discuss your project and embark on a journey towards a more beautiful and functional outdoor space.
